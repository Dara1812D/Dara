1. Soccer is the most popular sport in the world, with an estimated 4 billion fans worldwide.
2. The modern game of soccer originated in England in the 19th century, but similar games have been played in different cultures for centuries.
3. The FIFA World Cup, held every four years, is the biggest sporting event in the world. The 2018 World Cup was watched by an estimated 3.5 billion people.
4. The standard soccer ball is made up of 32 leather or synthetic leather panels, stitched together in a pattern that resembles a truncated icosahedron.
5. A regulation soccer match consists of two 45-minute halves, with a 15-minute halftime break.
6. The objective of the game is to score more goals than the opposing team by kicking the ball into their goal. A goal is scored when the entire ball crosses the goal line between the two goalposts and under the crossbar.
7. The only player on the field who is allowed to use their hands and arms is the goalkeeper, and only within their own penalty area.
8. Soccer is played at both the amateur and professional levels, with professional leagues in countries all around the world.
9. Some of the most successful soccer clubs in the world include Real Madrid, Barcelona, Manchester United, Bayern Munich, and Liverpool.
10.Soccer is known for its passionate fan culture, with supporters often singing chanting, and waving flags and banners to support their team.
